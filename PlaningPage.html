Purpose Statement

The mission of this website is to provide the view with weather, water, climate data, forecasts, and warnings for the prevention of damages and the protection of life, property and they effective use of time. As well as to help the local economy run smoothly. The audience I intend this website for is the people who live in Franklin, Greenville, and Springfield as well as any people who may be traveling through those areas.?

The site will accomplish this goal by having professional meteorologists look at and analyze weather patterns and then publish that information online in an easily accessible and effective manner. We will also work with other meteorologists to create an accurate big picture so we can report weather to our viewers. And finally we will provide weekly forecasts and announcements of incoming warnings to help our viewers.

Audience Description

The audience for my website is a very broad based, they are made up of 60% female and 40% male, the age is anywhere from teenagers to elderly, the level of education is varied as well from before highschool to PhD. The income also ranges from 20,000 to 100,000. The technological experience also varies. They mainly use mobiles but the PC is also a common way of visiting the site.

Audience Persona

The persona I choose to select is Jeremiah Gentry. Jeremiah is a Highschool student who checks the weather to figure out what clothes he should wear and what plans he should do. He makes no money, and a quote from him is "I use this website to figure out if its going to rain"
