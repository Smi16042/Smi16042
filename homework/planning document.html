Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to inform current league of legend players about some of the current tactics that works in the game.

Target Audience

The target audience is current League of legends players who have reached the rank of silver and gold, this encompases roughly 65% of the playerbase. Because the game is so popular and has so many fans defineing the audience is hard however most players are generally between the age of 15-25 and male. Keeping this in mind I will keep my comments short and full of data.

Example Persona

Cullins Edward is a 21 year old male college student, Cullins succesfully graduated from high school and is currently working towards a degree in business management, he uses League of Legends to connect with other people his age and relax.
Cullins has a fairly high level of technical expertise, he regularly uses a smart phone, laptop, and tablet. He enjoys browsing for new league websites during his lunch break and when he plays games at night when he's waiting for his game to que up.
Cullins would normally only spend 5-10 minutes per league website, he's looking for a site that will help him improve his skills and teach him tips and tricks that he doesnt know.
